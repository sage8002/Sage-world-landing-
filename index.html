Our Vision

To create a future where intelligent healthcare solutions are accessible, affordable, and personalized for every individual—regardless of location, background, or status.

Our Mission

To leverage the power of artificial intelligence to bridge healthcare gaps, empower medical professionals, and provide life-saving guidance to underserved populations worldwide.

Flagship Project: Sage AI

Sage AI is our AI-powered health assistant built for WhatsApp. It provides 24/7 medical guidance, triage support, health education, and referrals directly via chat—serving as a digital first responder in regions lacking reliable access to healthcare.

Future Projects

  • AI Medical Note Generator: Smart auto-documentation for doctors and clinics, saving time and reducing burnout.
  • Sage Pediatric-Ortho AI: Diagnostic tools for early detection of pediatric orthopedic issues, especially in developing countries.
  • Sage HealthHub: A unified platform connecting all Sage AI tools with user dashboards, analytics, and health records.
  • Smart Referral System: Integrated with Sage AI to direct users to trusted nearby clinics or specialists.
